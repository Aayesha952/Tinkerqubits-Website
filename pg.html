What We Do

Training Program

Tech for Teachers

Tech for Teacher is a dedicated teacher upskilling and capacity-building program designed to strengthen elementary computer science education by building teacher agency, confidence, and leadership. By empowering teachers as digital educators and leaders, Tech for Teacher ensures sustainable, high-quality computer science education—preparing students not just for today’s classrooms, but for future-ready STEM careers.

School Program

Tech for School

Tech for Schools is an in-school ( part of regular school computer period)program designed to strengthen elementary and secondary computer science education (Grades 1–10) by transforming how technology is taught and experienced by students. By integrating curriculum, teacher capacity, and infrastructure, Tech for Schools ensures children gain early, continuous, and equitable exposure to computer science—fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and a deeper understanding of growing in technology-driven world.

Annual Event

TMM (Thinker Maker's Mela)

Thinkers Makers Mela (TMM) is TinkerQubits Foundation’s annual flagship showcase and community-building event, bringing together students, educators, and technology enthusiasts to celebrate learning through creation. By emphasizing making, sharing, and reflection, Thinkers Makers Mela reinforces the journey from access to agency—showcasing how equitable digital education can spark innovation, ownership, and social change."

Community Program

Tech Tribe

Tech tribe Tech Tribes is a student leadership and peer-learning program designed to build sustainable, student-led technology communities. Structured as democratic, after-school clubs for students in Grades 6–9, the program nurtures agency, collaboration, and responsible technology use. By placing students at the center of learning and leadership, Tech Tribes cultivates conscious tech communities—empowering young people to use technology consciously, ethically and creatively for real-world.
